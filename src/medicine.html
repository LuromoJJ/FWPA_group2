Aspirin

+ Add to My Profile

Aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) is commonly used to reduce pain, fever, or inflammation. It can also help prevent heart attacks and strokes by reducing blood clotting. It should be taken with food or water and never on an empty stomach.
